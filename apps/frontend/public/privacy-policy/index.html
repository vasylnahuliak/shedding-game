Privacy Policy

Shedding Game

This Privacy Policy explains how Shedding Game handles information when you use the Shedding Game mobile app and companion website.

Last updated: April 1, 2026
Website: https://shedding-game.pp.ua/

Core account data Email address, display name, account ID, sign-in method, language preference, and optional emoji preferences.
Game and app activity Room participation, scores, winners, timestamps, invite-link activity, crash reports, and limited analytics events needed to operate and improve the service.
Your controls You can update profile details, change sign-in methods, and delete your account from the app settings.

1. Information we collect

  • Account and profile information. We collect information needed to create and maintain your account, including your email address, display name, account identifier, linked authentication providers, language preference, and optional emoji preferences.
  • Gameplay and multiplayer data. We process room membership, host and player status, game state, scores, winners, timestamps, and related game history so multiplayer games, statistics, and account features work correctly.
  • Analytics and diagnostics. We may collect app opens, screen views, authentication completion, lobby creation or joining, and game start or finish events. We also collect crash reports, stack traces, route names, app version, and platform details to monitor reliability and troubleshoot issues.
  • Invite and referral data. When invite links or deferred deep links are used, we may process short-link codes, domains, click identifiers, install referrer information on Android, or clipboard-based deferred deep-link data on iOS.
  • Local device storage. The app stores session data and small local state flags on your device so sign-in, password recovery, and deferred deep-link flows can work.

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
  • To create, host, join, and run multiplayer game rooms.
  • To show player profiles, statistics, and saved preferences.
  • To process invite links, deep links, and deferred install attribution flows.
  • To detect errors, investigate failures, and improve app stability and usability.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the service from abuse.

3. Data we do not request for core app use

Shedding Game does not request camera, microphone, contacts, or precise location permissions for its core gameplay and account features.

4. How information may be shared

  • Supabase for authentication and account session management.
  • Aptabase for product analytics and event measurement.
  • Sentry for crash reporting and error monitoring.
  • Smler for invite-link resolution, deferred deep-link handling, and conversion tracking.
  • Infrastructure providers that host the app, backend, and related services.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to enforce our terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

5. Retention and deletion

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain accounts, run games, and support legitimate business and legal needs.

If you delete your account, we remove your account record and authentication record. Some past gameplay records may be retained in de-identified or anonymized form, including replacing your visible name with Deleted user, so historical game data remains consistent.

6. Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without the consent, permissions, or other legal basis required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, please use the developer contact details in the relevant app store listing for Shedding Game to request review or deletion.

7. Your choices

  • You can update your display name and language preference inside the app.
  • You can manage linked sign-in methods and reset or change your password.
  • You can delete your account from the app settings.
  • You can also submit an account deletion request on the web at /account-deletion/.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page and update the date shown above.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the developer contact details provided in the app store listing or on the official Shedding Game website.

For account deletion requests outside the app, use /account-deletion/.
