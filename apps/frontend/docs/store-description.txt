Shed your hand, outplay your friends. Shedding Game brings the classic card game online — fast rounds, clever specials, and a Bridge mechanic that can flip the score in a single turn. Jump into a match in seconds, solo with bots or with friends around the world.


HOW IT PLAYS

Discard your cards by matching suit or rank. First to empty their hand wins the round — but every card left in opponents' hands adds points against them. Cross 125 points and you're eliminated. Last player standing takes the game.


SPECIAL CARDS

• 6 — Take another turn, any suit
• 7 — Next player draws 2 (or stacks another 7)
• 8 — Next player auto-draws 1 per eight played
• Jack — Wild card. Finish with jacks for -20 per opponent
• Ace — Skip the next player


THE BRIDGE

When the last four cards of the discard pile share the same rank, you can apply a Bridge — instantly ending the round and doubling every player's score. Time it right and you'll turn a losing hand into a game-winning move.
